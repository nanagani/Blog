Reveiw On The Movies

Avengers Endgame
The Avengers Endgame is a wonderful ending of the MCU.

I watched this moive in 2019 and I can still watch it today without being bored. As most people know, MCU intertwines all of it's movies, so to watch this movie you must watch most of the movies from before. Although it might be long, it is definetely worth the 3 hrs. This movie honors all of the marvel superheroes as they all come together to fight one true enemy. If you have been waiting after Avengers Endgame, then this will definetely help.

The Lion King
The Lion King is a good movie for animal lovers.

This movie is great, it is definetly a classic. However this movie was a little boring. The movie starts off as a young lion loses his father to his evil uncle. Watch as he goes on the journey to face his past.
